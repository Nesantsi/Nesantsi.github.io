Art Bot

Drawing

A fun senior year project I made with a classmate for my independent study computer science class. Wasn't really looking for a practical application of this, but it was fun to test my engineering and programming skills. It uses an edge detection program to convert images into a black and white outline, where the pixel coordinates are converted into motor commands allowing the machine to draw any picture.

Quadruped Robot

Quadruped Robot

Walking Gait

Inspired by Boston Dynamics's Big Dog robot, I created this quadruped robot to learn more about biomechanics and robotics. It utilizes an Arduino and 12 servo motors to move around. The program is a timed sequence of movements, and the robot doesn't have any sensors to detect and interpret what's around in its environment. I later hope to add pressure and ultrasonic senors to allow to navigate a room or uneven terrain and utilize inverse kinematics for leg movement.

Self Balancing Robot (To be improved)

Balancing robot

Balancing

A senior year project of mine that was inspired by the SpaceX Grasshopper rocket. I created a self righting platform as a first step in the direction of autonomous flight. It runs an accelerometer/gyroscope sensor utilizing a complementary filter to find its angle of orientation and then two servo motors to correct itself.
